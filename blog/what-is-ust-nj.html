What is UST NJ?

The name is intentionally boring. That is the point.

UST NJ = Underground Storage Tank operators for New Jersey.

We are a Class A/B operator service. If you have regulated fuel tanks in NJ and need a trained Class A and Class B operator on your NJDEP paperwork, that is the job.

People searching Google or asking AI “who can be my Class A/B operator in New Jersey” should land on something that says exactly that — not a vague consulting brand. UST NJ is built to be that answer.

What UST NJ does

  1. Audit your UST operator program (what’s fine, what’s broken)
  2. Put our licensed Class A/B on your Facility Certification Questionnaire
  3. Train Class C staff on site
  4. Run a simple program (calendar, records, call access)
  5. Help if problems hit (DEP letters, failures, retraining path)

What UST NJ does not do

  • We are not your tank installer or line tester (certified contractors do that)
  • We are not a law firm
  • We do not erase owner liability — NJ still holds owners/operators responsible

Who uses UST NJ

Gas stations, fleet fuel yards, card-locks, generator UST facilities, and multi-site owners across all 21 New Jersey counties.
