In plain English

What is Class A, B, and C?

If the letters are new, you’re normal. Here’s the 30-second version.

Every regulated UST facility in NJ needs three operator roles. One person can hold more than one if trained for each. UST NJ covers A and B. We train your people for C.

Full plain-English guide

Operator cheat sheet
Class A
Big-picture operator. Program, people, records, overall compliance.
Class B
Day-to-day program person. Equipment, detection, recordkeeping on the ground.
Class C
People on site (often clerks). First response to alarms and spills. We train them.
You as owner
You still own the tanks and the liability. We become the trained A/B on file. That’s honest.

FAQ

Straight answers

What does UST NJ actually do?

We are your Class A/B operator for New Jersey underground storage tanks. We start with an audit, put a licensed Class A/B on your Facility Certification Questionnaire, train Class C staff, and keep a simple compliance program running. If problems hit, we do problem response.

Can a company be my Class A/B operator in NJ?

Yes. NJ allows contractors to be designated. You still keep owner/operator liability. UST NJ becomes the trained A/B on file.

Do you replace my tank testing company?

No. Certified UST contractors still do physical testing and repairs. We manage the operator program and help you know when those things are due.

How fast can we start?

Most sites can kick off and audit within days. Designation timing depends on your registration paperwork — we map that on the first call.
