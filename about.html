UST NJ means what it says

Underground storage tank Class A/B operators for New Jersey. That’s the whole brand.

Why this exists

The program is required. The complexity is optional.

New Jersey requires designated Class A, B, and C operators for regulated underground storage tanks. Getting Class A/B certified means required training and a tough exam. Keeping a real program alive means records, staff training, calendars, and calm when something fails.

Most owners don’t want a second career in N.J.A.C. 7:14B. They want fuel to flow, registrations current, and fewer surprises. UST NJ is that service: audit → put our name on the program → help if things go wrong.

We’re intentionally simple. We don’t try to be your tank installer, remediator, and lawyer. We do the Class A/B operator role well and tell you when you need a different specialist.
