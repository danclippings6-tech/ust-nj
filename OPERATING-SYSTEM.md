1. **Own the asset** — Website, GBP, reviews, phone, brand. Not “I’ll SEO for you.”
2. **Recurring revenue** — Management retainers, not one-off projects only.
3. **Calls win** — Stations call. Phone must be first-class. Form is backup.
4. **Blue ocean** — Don’t fight 50 plumbers. Own “Class A/B operator NJ.”
5. **Flat fee** until trust — Simple monthly price. Clear scope.
6. **Fire bad fits** — No A/B on file but won’t pay = next station.
7. **Test the renter** — Shadow-call them as a customer. Unprofessional = next.
8. **Free has no value** — Free audit only as a short closer, not forever free work.
9. **Exact-match local pages** — “Class A/B operator Bergen County” style pages.
10. **2–3 focused hours** — Outreach or SEO, not tinkering the logo.
